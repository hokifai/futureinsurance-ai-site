Course Title: AI Innovation for Insurance Professionals

The insurance industry is undergoing rapid transformation driven by the rise of artificial intelligence. From underwriting to claims management, customer engagement to risk analytics, AI is redefining value creation in the insurance ecosystem. This course provides insurance professionals with the knowledge, tools, and applied skills to leverage AI technologies for innovation, efficiency, and competitive advantage.

Objectives

  • Equip participants with a foundational and strategic understanding of AI technologies and their applications in the insurance sector.
  • Develop the capability to apply AI for underwriting, risk modeling, fraud detection, claims management, and personalized insurance solutions.
  • Build practical skills in AI innovation using tools such as generative AI, Agentic AI, and custom AI modelling for insurance operations.
  • Foster a culture of responsible and ethical AI adoption aligned with regulatory, governance, and ESG considerations.
