Course Title: AI Innovation for Insurance Professionals

Introduction

This course provides insurance professionals with the knowledge, tools, and applied skills to leverage AI technologies for innovation, efficiency, and competitive advantage.

Objectives

  • Equip participants with a foundational understanding of AI technologies.
  • Develop the capability to apply AI for underwriting and fraud detection.
  • Build practical skills in AI innovation using Agentic AI models.

Course Structure

Structured Program (36 hours)

  1. Fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence in Insurance
  2. Generative AI and Large Language Models (LLMs)
  3. Agentic AI: The Next Frontier in Operations
  4. Custom AI Modelling for Underwriting
  5. AI for Claims Management and Fraud Detection
  6. Ethical and Responsible AI in Insurance
  7. Capstone Project: Innovation Roadmap
