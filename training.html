Course Title: AI Innovation for Insurance Professionals

The insurance industry is undergoing rapid transformation driven by the rise of artificial intelligence. From underwriting to claims management, customer engagement to risk analytics, AI is redefining value creation in the insurance ecosystem. This course provides insurance professionals with the knowledge, tools, and applied skills to leverage AI technologies for innovation, efficiency, and competitive advantage.

01 Objectives

  • • Equip participants with a foundational and strategic understanding of AI technologies.
  • • Develop the capability to apply AI for underwriting, risk modeling, and fraud detection.
  • • Build practical skills in generative AI, Agentic AI, and custom modelling.
  • • Foster a culture of responsible and ethical AI adoption (ESG).

02 Learning Outcomes

  • • Explain business value of Agentic AI in insurance.
  • • Apply AI techniques to enhance operational efficiency.
  • • Design use cases involving custom AI models.
  • • Integrate responsible AI regulatory frameworks.
