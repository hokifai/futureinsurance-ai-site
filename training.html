Course Title: AI Innovation for Insurance Professionals

The insurance industry is undergoing rapid transformation driven by the rise of artificial intelligence. From underwriting to claims management, customer engagement to risk analytics, AI is redefining value creation in the insurance ecosystem.

Objectives

  • Equip participants with a foundational and strategic understanding of AI technologies.
  • Develop the capability to apply AI for underwriting, risk modeling, and fraud detection.
  • Build practical skills in AI innovation using tools such as generative AI and Agentic AI.
  • Foster a culture of responsible and ethical AI adoption.

Course Structure

Structured Program (36 hours)

  • Fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence in Insurance
  • Data Strategy and Digital Transformation
  • Generative AI and Large Language Models (LLMs)
  • Agentic AI: The Next Frontier
  • Capstone Project: AI Innovation Roadmap
